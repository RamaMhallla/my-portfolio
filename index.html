Research project focused on time-dependent prediction of protein molecular dynamics using deep learning models.   • Time-series prediction of molecular dynamics trajectories using deep neural networks
  • Modeling high-dimensional sequential data (coordinates, velocities, forces)
  • Dimensionality reduction for long-term temporal prediction
  • Learning-based acceleration of molecular simulations
  • Exploration of uncertainty, stability, and generalization in dynamical systems
